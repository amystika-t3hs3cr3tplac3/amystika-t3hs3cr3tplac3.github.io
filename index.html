What is Amystika?

Amystika is a minimalistic marketplace and gallery of Bitcoin NFTs.
Bitcoin NFTs will be revealed here in seasons.
Each season will come with new features and a re-vamp of the website.

We are starting with season 0 where we will reveal 100 Bitcoin NFTs.
For season 0, only a portion of the whole art piece will be revealed.
The complete art piece will be revealed when the NFT is minted.

From season 1 and onwards, NFTs from other artists will be revealed on Amystika.

Reach out to us on X or amystika.art@proton.me to be featured.
FAQ

Will there be support for wallets?

Yes, we are working on bringing support for wallets from Season 1.

How can I buy an NFT?

At the moment in season 0, all NFTs will be minted via their associated mint address. Send the required amount of Bitcoins by signing and broadcasting the PSBT or by manually sending the required amount to the mint address.
NOTE: MAKE SURE YOU ARE SENDING THE BITCOINS FROM A TAPROOT ADDRESS

Why do I have to send the amount from a taproot address?

All NFTs that are bought will be sent to the buyer's Bitcoin address. As Bitcoin NFTs (Ordinal Inscriptions) can only be stored in the taproot, the buyer needs to send the required amount from a taproot address to be able to successfully receive the NFT.

I accidentally sent the amount from a non-taproot address. Can I get the Bitcoins back?

Yes, all significant amount of satoshis accidentally sent from a non-taproot address will be returned.

I accidentally sent the wrong amount to the mint address. Can I get the Bitcoins back?

Yes, all significant amount of satoshis not meeting the mint price accidentally sent to the mint address will be returned.
